Fresh Pasta and Healthy salad

A delightful blend of fluffy quinoa, fresh vegetables, and protein-rich chickpeas, dressed in a zesty lemon vinaigrette. Perfect for a healthy lunch or light dinner.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa 1 cup
  • Chickpeas 1 can
  • Cherry tomatoes 1 cup
  • Cucumber 1 medium
  • Avocado 1 large
  • Olive oil 2 tbsp
  • Lemon juice 1 tbsp

Preparation

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly and cook according to package instructions. Usually 2 parts water to 1 part quinoa for about 15-20 minutes until fluffy.
  2. While quinoa is cooking, drain and rinse chickpeas. Slice cherry tomatoes in half and dice cucumber into small cubes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Once quinoa is cooked, let it cool for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chickpeas, tomatoes, and cucumber.
  6. Pour the dressing over the mixture and toss gently to combine.
  7. Just before serving, slice the avocado and arrange on top of each portion.
